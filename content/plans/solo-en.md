## FAQ

**Can I switch teams after signing up?**
Yes. You can change your selected team once per month, effective the following month. Your knowledge base carries over.

**What happens after the 3-month commitment?**
You continue month-to-month. No commitment. Cancel anytime with no penalty and no notice period.

**Can I upgrade to Studio or Agency later?**
Yes. Upgrade anytime. Your knowledge base, brand context, and all past work transfer to the new plan.

**What if I need a task outside my team's scope?**
You can add any additional team for EUR 190/month. Or upgrade to Studio for 3 teams at EUR 990/month.

**How do I communicate with my team?**
Email. Send your task to your dedicated team address. You get deliverables back. No platform, no login, no app to install.

**Is the setup fee really waived?**
For the first 10 founding clients, yes. The EUR 490 setup fee is completely waived. After that, it applies as a one-time charge.

**What does the first-week calibration look like?**
You share your brand guidelines, a few examples of content you like, and your immediate priorities. We build your knowledge base from that. Takes about 30 minutes of your time total.
